## Why this exists

For most of my career I&rsquo;ve done my thinking in slide decks, business
cases, and customer workshops. This journal is the public-facing version of
that — short, honest notes on the things I work on, the things I love, and
the messy middle between them.

## What to expect

Three flavors of writing, in rough order of frequency:

- **Enterprise strategy** — outcome-based selling, value engineering, how
  large customers actually buy and operate technology.
- **Adoption & operations** — what it really takes to turn a platform
  purchase into measurable value.
- **The night sky** — astronomy outreach, observing notes, occasional
  astrophotography, and stories from Houston Astronomical Society / Texas
  Star Party / Solar System Ambassador work.

Posts will be short. I&rsquo;d rather ship a useful 400-word note than sit on
a 2,000-word essay forever.
